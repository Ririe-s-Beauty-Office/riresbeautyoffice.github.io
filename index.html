About Ririe's Beauty Office

Your Beauty Destination

Welcome to Ririe's Beauty Office, where passion meets perfection. With over 7 years of experience in the beauty industry, we've dedicated ourselves to making every client feel beautiful, confident, and pampered.

Our founder, Ririe, is a certified beauty professional with expertise in nail artistry, skincare, makeup application, and various beauty treatments. Her vision was to create a warm, welcoming space where everyone can experience premium beauty services.

We use only the highest quality products and stay updated with the latest beauty trends and techniques to ensure you receive the best service possible. Your satisfaction and safety are our top priorities.

At Ririe's Beauty Office, we believe that beauty is personal, and every service is customized to enhance your natural features and boost your confidence.

Yes, we recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot. Walk-ins are welcome subject to availability, but appointments ensure you won't have to wait.

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. Payment is due at the time of service.
How long do lash extensions last? ‚ñº
Lash extensions typically last 3-4 weeks with proper care. We recommend coming in for fills every 2-3 weeks to maintain the full look.
What is your cancellation policy? ‚ñº
We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

Yes! We use high-quality, professional products suitable for all skin types. If you have allergies or sensitivities, please inform us during booking so we can accommodate your needs.
Do you offer bridal packages? ‚ñº
Absolutely! We offer comprehensive bridal packages including makeup trials, beauty treatments, and group bookings. Contact us to discuss your special day requirements.

While we have an extensive collection of colors, you're welcome to bring your own polish. However, we use professional-grade products that ensure the best results and longevity.
